CHAPTER 28: THE MERCENARY HEIRESS
Seo-Jun didn't flinch at the blade. He looked at the woman—beautiful, lethal, and draped in the aura of a killer. He remembered his father mentioning her: Hana, the girl the President adopted and raised as his 'Shadow successor' while Seo-Jun was kept in the dark.
Seo-Jun "You're just a tool, Hana. An adopted weapon. I am the rightful owner by blood. This house, this empire... it was built on my mother’s grave. You have no claim here except the one my father gave you."
Hana stepped closer, the red light catching the cold steel of the knife she had just retrieved. She didn't look like a sister; she looked like a Queen deciding whether or not to execute a prisoner.
Hana "Blood is just a liquid, 'Prince.' Power is what matters. Dad gave me the codes, the keys, and the command of the guards. You have the signatures... but I have the physical control. If I press one button, your 'Audit' footage disappears forever."

Hana "Dad wants us to fight for the throne. He wants to see which wolf is stronger. But why should we kill each other? You have the blood, I have the steel. If we merge... there won't be a Board or a President left to stop us."

Hana "I'm proposing we take this house together. And once we do... I’ll show you why Dad chose me to be his greatest weapon."
