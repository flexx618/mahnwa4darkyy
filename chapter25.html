CHAPTER 25: THE FINAL BOARDROOM
KIM MANSION // THE DINING HALL // THE AMBUSH
The heavy oak doors swung open. Directors Min, Choi, and Park walked in, laughing, adjusting their silk ties and smelling of expensive cologne. Director Min was carrying a bottle of vintage champagne, ready to toast the end of the Kim Dynasty.
Director Min "Shefiee! We saw the car outside. Is it done? Has the 'orphan' been packed away yet? Let's sign the dissolution and—"
Min’s voice trailed off. He saw Shefiee slumped in her chair, her eyes red, her hands shaking. He saw Gobo, the son of their 'cleaner', standing like a guard. And he saw Seo-Jun, standing at the head of the table like he already owned the room.
Director Choi "What is the meaning of this? Why is the boy still here? Shefiee, speak up!"
** CLICK — PLAY — REPLAY **
The wall monitor flickered to life. Not just one screen, but four. Each one showed a different 'Audit.' Screen 1: Rhea in the washroom. Screen 2: Director Min’s daughter in the garden. Screen 3: Choi’s niece at the base camp. Seo-Jun had been busy. He hadn't just 'audited' the family; he had systematically compromised every single bloodline on the Board.
Seo-Jun "Welcome, Directors. Please, have a seat. I’ve already prepared the entertainment for today. It’s a highlight reel of your family’s... dedication to my legacy."
CRITICAL STATUS: TOTAL EXPOSURE

The Directors frozen in shock. The 'Safe' investments they thought they had were nothing compared to the 4K proof of their daughters' submission. Combined with the Dashcam audio of the accident playing in the background, the room felt like a tomb.

Gobo "Thirteen years ago, you all took a piece of the payout for the 'accident.' My father's diary has every name. Every bank account. Every dirty cent. You aren't here to vote. You're here to surrender."
Seo-Jun slid a single, thick document across the table. It required four signatures. Four lives. One empire.
Seo-Jun "Sign the full transfer. If one pen stays dry, I hit 'Upload' on all four servers. Your daughters will be the most famous faces on the internet by lunch, and the police will be at your doors by dinner. Choose wisely."
** SCRATCH — SCRATCH — SCRATCH **
One by one, the Directors fell. The arrogance was gone, replaced by the desperate scratching of pens on paper. As the last Director, Park, signed his name, the 'Adopted Rule' was no longer a secret. It was the only law left in the Kim Mansion.
