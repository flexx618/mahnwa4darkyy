CHAPTER 28: THE SOVEREIGN MERGER
The red emergency lighting didn't just illuminate the room; it stained it. Hana’s proposal hung in the air, thick with the scent of ozone and something far more primal. She wasn't just offering a deal; she was testing the weight of the man standing before her.
Hana "I'm proposing we take this house together. And once we do... I’ll show you why Dad chose me to be his greatest weapon."
Seo-Jun felt the shift. The "Sister" he thought he had was a myth. Standing before him was a predator who shared his ambition, but lacked his blood. He reached out, his hand closing over hers on the hilt of the knife.
Seo-Jun "A merger, Hana? Or a Contract? You have the keys to the security, but without my blood, the Board will never truly bow. You’re the 'Power,' but I’m the 'Legitimacy.' Alone, we’re pawns. Together... we own the chessboard."
** THUD — HEARTBEAT — HEAT **
Hana’s eyes searched his. She wasn't looking for kindness; she was testing his loyalty. She saw the "True Blood" fire in him—the same fire that burned her when she was forced to "audit" the Board's failures.
Hana "You want a Contract? Fine. Let’s sign it in the only way this family understands. A marriage of steel and blood. You keep your Board, and I keep my army. We rule this mansion as King and Queen... and our first 'Audit' will be the man who tried to play us against each other."
THE UNHOLY ALLIANCE

Hana realizes that Seo-Jun is the only man capable of taming her. Seo-Jun realizes Hana is the only woman capable of protecting his back. The 'Romance' is built on mutual destruction.

She pulled him closer, her tactical vest pressing against his chest. The sexual tension was no longer a subtext; it was a weapon. She whispered against his lips, her breath hot in the chilled room.
Hana "The Board thinks they’re being audited. They don't realize the 'Trial' is actually our Engagement. If you can take that knife from the table without breaking my grip... I’ll give you the master override for the entire Kim network."
Seo-Jun didn't pull. He twisted. With a sudden burst of strength, he pinned her hand against the mahogany, his face inches from hers. The "Weapon" had met its "Master."
Seo-Jun "Consider the override mine. Now, let’s go show Dad what happens when his two greatest creations decide to stop killing each other... and start killing him."
