CHAPTER 32: THE HOUSE OF SURRENDER
The rain continued to wash over the Kim estate, but for Rhea, it felt like the first clean air she had breathed in a decade. She stood by the fountain, clutching the Diary and the SD card Seo-Jun had provided. The truth was heavy, but the weight of the mystery had been heavier.
Seo-Jun "Sheffie is in custody. The Board is dismantled. You have the proof, Rhea. You have the shares that belonged to your mother's lineage. You could sit at this table. You could lead with us."
Rhea looked at the massive, dark mansion behind them. To Seo-Jun, it was a throne. To her, it was a mausoleum. She looked back at Seo-Jun and Hana, her eyes clear for the first time since the accident.
She tucked the diary into her coat. A black sedan, unmarked and waiting, pulled up to the gate. It wasn't a company car. It was a getaway.
Hana "You're leaving. Just like that?"
Rhea "I’m the only one here who still knows how to live a life without a weapon in my hand. Keep the company. Keep the war. I’m going to go find the version of me that didn't die on that highway."
** ENGINE START — TIRES ON GRAVEL **
Without a backward glance, Rhea entered the car. As the tail lights disappeared into the mist
The heavy iron gates of the estate hissed shut, locking the world out. Inside, the purge was complete, but the mansion remained populated. In the grand parlor, three women stood trembling: Mrs. Lisa, the stepmother whose power evaporated with her husband's arrest, and her two daughters, May and Unisan.
Mrs. Lisa "Seo-Jun... please. We had no part in the Board's decisions. We didn't know about the accident. You can't cast us out... we have nothing left."
Seo-Jun "I'm not casting you out, Lisa. In fact, I've decided you'll all be staying. You aren't my family—there is no blood between us—but you are part of this house. And everything in this house belongs to me now."
He stepped closer to May and Unisan. They flinched, but they didn't look away. They knew their survival depended on the whim of the man standing before them.
Seo-Jun "You'll live in luxury. You'll have the finest things. But you will have to serve your bodies to the King."
Hana "Consider it a promotion. You went from being the daughters of a corrupt regime to being the private collection of the man who conquered it. Don't make me remind you of the alternative."
** SNAP — THE DOOR LOCKS **
RESULT: DOMESTIC DOMINION

The step-family has been stripped of agency. May, Unisan, and Lisa are now permanent fixtures of Seo-Jun's private court. Without blood ties or legal standing, their roles have shifted from heirs to property.

Seo-Jun "Lisa, take the girls to the East Wing. I'll be visiting later tonight to establish the new 'household protocols.' Hana... let's go. The Boardroom is waiting."
As the three women retreated in silence, Seo-Jun looked at Hana. The game was no longer about survival. It was about absolute, uninhibited satisfaction.
