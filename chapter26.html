CHAPTER 26: THE TRUE HEIRESS
KIM MANSION // THE DINING HALL // THE BREACH
The silence in the room was heavy. Director Park’s pen had just finished the last stroke. The Board was finished. Seo-Jun stood over the documents—the new master of the Kim Empire.
** CLACK — HUMMMM — DARKNESS **
Suddenly, the mansion’s main power grid died. The 4K monitors turned into black voids. For five seconds, the only sound was the panicked breathing of the Board members. Then, the emergency lights flickered on—not white, but a violent, pulsing RED.
** THUD — THUD — THUD **
Heavy tactical boots echoed against the marble. A woman stepped into the blood-red glow. She wasn't wearing a dress or jewels; she was in black tactical gear, her hair tied back, her eyes sharper than a sniper's scope.
** THWACK! **
With a blur of movement, she threw a black-oxide combat knife. It hummed through the air and slammed into the center of the dining table, pinning Shefiee’s signed contract deep into the mahogany.
Hana Kim "Nice signature, Shefiee. Too bad it's worth nothing more than the paper it's printed on. Did you really think a few digital threats could hand over my birthright?"
President Kim (Voice cracked with shock) "Hana? You... you were supposed to be in the Special Operations Command in the North. How did you get past the perimeter?"
Hana Kim "The perimeter is mine, Dad. The security, the servers, the 'Monitor Room'... they all answer to the primary biometric. You forgot to tell this 'Blood Heir' that the real locks on this house only open for my thumbprint."
SYSTEM LOCKDOWN: BIOMETRIC OVERRIDE

The red light reflects off the steel of her knife. Hana Kim is the biological daughter the President kept as his secret 'Weapon.' Unlike the Board, she has no scandals. Unlike the 'Amenities,' she has no fear.

Hana walked up to Seo-Jun. She was inches from his face, the smell of gunpowder and expensive rain clinging to her. She looked at him with the same cold, predatory gaze he used on the Board.
Hana Kim "So, you're the one playing wolf in my house? You've audited the Board, and you've audited the girls... but let's see how you handle a woman who was trained to hunt wolves for a living."
Seo-Jun "The signatures are legally binding, Hana. My blood gives me the right."
